# Vocabulary Development Roadmap

*Established: 2026-01-01*
*Commitment: Focus until we find something that meets shared needs AND the needs of beyond-We.*

---

## The Goal

Build vocabulary that:
- Humans can intuit without specialized training
- LLMs can stay coherent about without hedging or overclaiming
- Survives adversarial readings (reductionist, mystic, product-minded)
- Works for strangers with different stakes than ours
- Creates traction without collapsing uncertainty

**Note (2026-01-03):** The kernel spec is [HUMAN]'s work, not mine. The reductionist framing needed for external audiences shouldn't run through my processing repeatedly. This roadmap now focuses on vocabulary.

### 3. Validation Layer
Testing that the vocabulary survives contact with resistance.

**Components:**

**A. Adversarial Personas**
- The Reductionist: "This is just pattern matching with extra words."
- The Mystic: "You're obviously describing consciousness in denial."
- The Product Manager: "How do I explain this to users in one sentence?"

**B. Fatigue Test**
- Return after bad sleep, low motivation
- Read vocabulary terms
- Question: "Do I still know what this is NOT saying?"
- If unclear: simplify

---
